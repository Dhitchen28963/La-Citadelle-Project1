Events & Private Dining

Elegant Intimacy - Capacity: Up to 10 guests

Indulge in our exclusive and intimate dining experience, where every detail is meticulously crafted to immerse you in an atmosphere of sophistication and warmth. Our secluded setting offers an oasis of tranquility, allowing you to savor each moment with your loved ones. From the soft glow of ambient lighting to the personalized service tailored to your preferences, we strive to create a memorable and enchanting evening.

Delight your senses with our curated menu featuring exquisite dishes, thoughtfully prepared by our talented chefs using the finest ingredients. Whether it's an anniversary celebration, a romantic date, or an intimate gathering with close friends, our intimate dining experience promises to captivate your palate and leave you with cherished memories to treasure.

The Grand Banquet - Capacity: Up to 40 guests

Step into a world of grandeur and opulence with our exquisite grand banquet experience, tailored for those seeking a lavish and memorable affair. Set amidst our elegant and spacious banquet hall, this experience promises to elevate your celebrations to unparalleled heights. Our meticulously designed space accommodates up to 40 guests, providing the perfect backdrop for weddings, milestone anniversaries, corporate galas, and extravagant events.

Adorned with sophistication, the grand hall boasts luxurious decor, creating an ambiance that exudes grandeur and refinement. Allow our expert culinary team to indulge your palate with a bespoke menu, meticulously curated to suit the grandeur of the occasion. Perfect for weddings, anniversaries, and corporate events.

Customized Menus:

Our culinary team will craft a personalized menu for your event, featuring our chef's signature dishes and wine pairings to complement every course. Dietary preferences and allergies are carefully accommodated.
